Internet of Things

With this application you can check and control all your devices present in your network.

Here you will be able to check:

  • What sensor are publishing into the network.
  • What actuator are reading from sensors.

Actuator

Actuator is the device that reads a data from server and takes an action based on it.

Sensor

Sensor is the device responsable for collecting data from the enviroment and publishing its data to the server.
